    Changing the colour palette

    Changing the colour palette based on whether a user is on a clinician or employer page enhanced the user experience and helped users orient themselves quickly, reinforcing content relevance, and clarity.

    Navbar states

    We aimed to reduce confusion by displaying only the content relevant to whether a user is logged in as a clinician or an employer. By streamlining the experience, we enhance clarity and ensure users see only what's relevant to them.

    Using cookies and other development magic we were able to direct the user to their relevant home page. Employer groups were brought directly to their overview dashboard once they entered ivyclinicians.io and clinicians were shown the search bar to easily continue their job search.
