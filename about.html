How I work

🫶
User-centered design

I lead projects from concept to delivery, ensuring every design stage is user-centered and impactful. By defining vision, mapping workflows, and refining details, I solve real user problems through research and interviews, creating seamless experiences that balance user needs and business goals.

🔑
Clear communication

I create functional prototypes that translate vision into reality, communicating value to stakeholders and functionality to developers. Collaborating closely with engineers and leveraging my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I ensure seamless handoffs and deliver innovative, feasible solutions.

💭
Genuine insights

I believe in using flexible, iterative research processes. By conducting informal user testing and leveraging a variety of methodologies, I gather actionable insights without making the process feel strained or forced. This agility ensures the end product is both authentic, intuitive and effective.

Toptal designer

As a Toptal designer, I’m part of an exclusive network representing the top 3% of design talent worldwide. This distinction is a result of rigorous vetting, ensuring that I bring expertise, creativity, and problem-solving skills to every project. Working with world-class companies on a wide range of projects has honed my ability to tackle complex challenges and collaborate seamlessly with teams.

FAQs

What services do you offer?
I thrive on variety, tackling everything from building new digital design products from scratch to breathing fresh life into existing apps, websites, and software. I take a hands-on role across the entire design journey—whether it’s diving into user research, shaping product strategy, defining branding, designing intuitive interfaces, or prototyping solutions. Every step is about creating meaningful, user and business focused outcomes
What timezones do you work in?
I’m based in Lisbon (GMT timezone) and am happy to take on projects with at least a 2-hour overlap in working hours (9am - 6pm).
What makes your approach to UX/UI design unique?
My approach is rooted in a deep understanding of user behavior, business goals, and technical feasibility. I don’t just design for aesthetics, I focus on solving real problems through research, testing, and iteration. By balancing creativity with strategy, I deliver solutions that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive, functional, and aligned with your objectives.
Do you offer development?
While I have a foundational understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I wouldn’t confidently take on a development project entirely from scratch. That said, I’ve built and launched websites using tools like Webflow and Framer, and I feel proficient enough to independently manage projects using these platforms.
What industries have you worked in?
I've worked across industries like healthcare, fintech, education, music and gaming, giving me a broad perspective on diverse user needs and business challenges. This experience allows me to quickly adapt to new industries, identify patterns, and apply best practices to create tailored solutions that drive results for your specific audience.
